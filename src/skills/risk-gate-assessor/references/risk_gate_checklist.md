# Risk gate checklist and evidence signals

Use these required elements and evidence signals when performing risk gate assessments.

## 1. Project scope is clear, validated; SOW has been shared
Evidence signals: statement of work, signed or shared SOW, scope boundaries, in-scope and out-of-scope items, validated scope, assumptions, deliverables, acceptance criteria, commercial scope alignment.

## 2. Reviewed Client Readiness Checklist
This element requires evidence that the checklist has been reviewed and all eight sub-actions have been performed or explicitly addressed:
1. Determine process for access to client systems.
2. Obtain project org chart from client.
3. Discuss DevOps or build management tool.
4. Review project methodology with executive team and key stakeholders.
5. Obtain current business process flows.
6. Retrieve list of applications/systems and product list/catalog.
7. For change management activities, determine participants for interviews, surveys, job shadowing, as applicable.
8. Determine sources of data and whether there is a data warehouse, including data quality and structure.

## 3. Sales-to-delivery handoff completed
Look for evidence that Sales met with Delivery or provided a structured handoff covering the following topics: client context, pursuit history, solution scope, Salesforce architecture, delivery roadmap, global process standardization, discovery timeline, workshops, deliverables, team roles, financial controls, risks, security, logistics, ways of working planning, and high-performing team norms.

## 4. Delivery leadership introduced to client leadership and SOW reviewed
Evidence signals: leadership introductions, executive alignment meeting, client sponsor meeting, delivery lead or engagement lead introduction, SOW walkthrough, SOW review with client leadership.

## 5. Alignment with third-party vendors if needed
Evidence signals: vendor alignment plan, SI/vendor coordination, third-party responsibilities, vendor dependencies, integration vendor meetings, vendor RACI, explicit not-applicable decision.

## 6. Staff needed for kickoff and discovery assigned
Evidence signals: named project team, staffing plan, kickoff attendees assigned, discovery team assigned, role roster, resource plan, confirmed availability.

## 7. Discover kickoff materials complete
Requires a kickoff deck or equivalent material with details on: team roles and introductions, project objectives and benefits, project scope, high-level timeline, methodology overview and risk gates, discover phase details, discovery deliverables, project meetings and communications, immediate next steps, and detailed schedule.

## 8. Discover workshop prep and sessions scheduled
Requires a document that contains details of planned workshops with dates and topics. Evidence signals: workshop calendar, discovery schedule, session plan, agenda by topic, participant list, facilitator, dates.

## 9. Engagement Pulse set up
Evidence signals: Engagement Pulse setup, pulse survey, project health pulse, team sentiment cadence, pulse owner, pulse launch or planned cadence.

## 10. Program governance planned and initiated
Requires a document that contains governance process initiatives, roles and responsibilities, individual project roles, and governance at a glance. Evidence signals: governance model, steering committee, cadence, decision rights, escalation path, RACI, program governance deck, governance calendar.

## 11. Licensing secured wherever needed
Evidence signals: Salesforce licenses, sandbox licenses, tool licenses, environment access, license procurement, license assignment, explicit licensing not applicable statement.
